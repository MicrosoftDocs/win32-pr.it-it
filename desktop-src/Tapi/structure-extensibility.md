data-stu-id="3dd15-106">In constants that are scalar enumerations, a range of values is reserved for future common extensions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3dd15-107">Il resto dei valori viene identificato come specifico del dispositivo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3dd15-107">The remainder of values is identified as device specific.</span></span> <span
