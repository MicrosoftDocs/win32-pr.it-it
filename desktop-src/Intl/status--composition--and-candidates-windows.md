<span data-ttu-id="55102-111">L'IME invia i caratteri composti all'applicazione in grado di riconoscere IME sotto forma di messaggi di risultato/GCS di [**WM \_ IME \_ char**](wm-ime-char.md) o [**WM \_ IME \_ Composition**](wm-ime-composition.md) \_ .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="55102-111">The IME sends the composed characters to the IME-aware application in the form of [**WM\_IME\_CHAR**](wm-ime-char.md) or [**WM\_IME\_COMPOSITION**](wm-ime-composition.md)/GCS\_RESULT messages.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="55102-112">Se l'applicazione non elabora questi messaggi, la funzione [**DefWindowProc**](/windows/desktop/api/winuser/nf-winuser-defwindowproca) li converte in uno o più messaggi [**WM \_ char**](../inputdev/wm-char.md) .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="55102-112">If the application does not process these messages, the [**DefWindowProc**](/windows/desktop/api/winuser/nf-winuser-defwindowproca) function translates them into one or more [**WM\_CHAR**](../inputdev/wm-char.md) messages.</span></span>
